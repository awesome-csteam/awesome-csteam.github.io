AWESOME Team

Dima Rabadi OK is a Research Scientist at the Cybersecurity Department of the Institute for Infocomm Research (I2R), Singapore. She received her Ph.D. (Oct. 2018) degree from the Singapore University of Technology and Design and the B.S. (June 2014) degree in Network Engineering and Security of Computers from the Jordan University of Science and Technology. Her research interests primarily lie in cybersecurity, digital forensics, and computer networks and security.

Sin G. Teo is a Research Scientist at the Cybersecurity Department of the Institute for Infocomm Research (I2R), Singapore. He obtained his Ph.D. degree from Monash University, Australia, in 2016. His research interests include applied cryptography, data privacy and security, privacy-preserving technologies, data mining, deep learning, and malware detection and classification.

Jia Y. Loo is a Research Engineer at the Cybersecurity Department of the Institute for Infocomm Research (I2R), Singapore. She received her B.Eng (Aug 2018) degree in Electrical & Electronic Engineering from Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. Her research interests include Machine Learning applications in Cybersecurity fields.
